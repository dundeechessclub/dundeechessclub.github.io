TAFCA 28/10

Posted on 30th October 2024 by Ray Flood

Division 1

Ed’s loss the only surprise, but a fine performance from Alastair. The A Team start as they left off in the pursuit of a 12th consecutive title.

Division 2

A fine win against a strong Stonehaven side who are likely to be a big challenger this season. A highly unusual slip from Gordon, but Bruce and I managed good wins. Keith won’t begrudge the verdict that he had a bit of fortune (?!) against the unfortunate Stonehaven Bruce.

But an excellent start to the C Team’s campaign.
