TAFCA & Club Championship 13/1/25

Posted on 17th January 2025 by Ray Flood

A tough fixture expected and it certainly materialised. Did it really all come down to four wins for White?!

Adam fell astray in a typically sharp Keith Rose attack against his Caro Kan. Jim’s game was also typical in a different way as he ground down Trevor in the ending. Alastair grabbed an early initiative against untypical innacuracies from Robert, and seized the advantage against his weakened squares and pawns. It was left to Josh and Andy to fight for a win/draw in the last game, and Josh was clearly better right up to the early middlegame where he feels he suffered an aneurysm. An unfortunate slide, but we’ve all been there.

The B Team look safe enough in Division 1, despite ruing the dropped point.

Club Championship Round 1

Gavin Boyd 0-1 Bill Boyd 1564

A solid opening from both players until Bill took control. Gavin being given the lesson “Don’t pin your opponent’s King Bishop before they’ve castled”!
